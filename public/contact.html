How to Contact Us

In-App Feedback Widget

Visit glowmi.org and click the feedback button (bottom-right corner). You can send messages, attach screenshots, and describe issues directly. This is the fastest way to reach us.

Response Time

We review all feedback regularly and typically respond within 48 hours during business days.

What You Can Contact Us About

General Questions

Have a question about how to use Glowmi's AI beauty tools, skin type quiz, or any other feature? Send us a message through the feedback widget and we will get back to you as soon as possible. You can also check our blog for detailed guides and how-to articles.

Feature Suggestions

We are constantly improving Glowmi and adding new features based on user feedback. If you have an idea for a new tool, analysis type, or content category, we would love to hear about it. Please include as much detail as possible about what you would like to see and how it would help your K-Beauty journey.

Content Corrections

We strive for accuracy in all our content, including clinic information, procedure details, product recommendations, and ingredient data. If you notice any outdated or incorrect information — whether it is a clinic that has closed, a price that has changed, or a product that has been reformulated — please let us know and we will update it promptly. Accurate information is our top priority.

Bug Reports

If you encounter a technical issue with the website, such as an AI tool not loading, a feature not working as expected, or a display problem on your device, please let us know with the following details:

  • What you were trying to do
  • What happened instead
  • Your device and browser (e.g., iPhone 15, Safari)
  • A screenshot if possible (you can attach one in the feedback widget)

Partnership Inquiries

Are you a K-Beauty brand, dermatology clinic, or content creator interested in working with Glowmi? We are open to collaborations that benefit our community. Please reach out through the feedback widget with details about your organization and how you envision working together.

Note on AI Analysis: Our AI beauty tools (Personal Color, Face Shape, Skin Analyzer) provide estimates for informational purposes only. They are not medical diagnoses. For skin concerns, we recommend consulting a licensed dermatologist. See our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy for more details.

Data Requests

If you would like to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data, you can use the "Delete All My Data" button on the My Page tab within the app. This permanently removes all your data from our database.

For full details on how we handle your data, please see our Privacy Policy.
