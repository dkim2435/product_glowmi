Glowmi 소개 · Your Personal K-Beauty Companion

Glowmi (Glow + Me) is your personal K-Beauty companion, designed to help beginners and enthusiasts alike navigate the world of Korean skincare and beauty. Whether you are exploring K-Beauty for the first time, building a personalized skincare routine, or planning a trip to Seoul for aesthetic treatments, our goal is to provide clear, accurate, and helpful information in both English and Korean.

We believe that everyone deserves access to personalized beauty guidance — without expensive consultations or confusing jargon. That is why every tool on Glowmi is completely free, requires no app download, and works directly in your browser.

What We Offer

AI Personal Color Analysis

Upload a selfie and discover your personal color type from 10 detailed categories (Spring Warm, Summer Cool, Autumn Warm, Winter Cool — each with 2-3 subtypes). Powered by Google Gemini AI, results arrive in under 3 seconds with a complete color palette, makeup shade recommendations, and styling tips. No sign-up required.

AI Face Shape Detection

Our AI analyzes 468 facial landmarks to determine your face shape from 7 types (oval, round, square, heart, oblong, diamond, triangle). Get personalized hairstyle recommendations, glasses frame suggestions, and makeup contouring tips tailored to your unique facial geometry.

AI Skin Condition Analyzer

Get your skin scored across 5 key metrics — redness, oiliness, dryness, dark spots, and texture — each on a 0-100 scale. Receive personalized improvement tips and track your skin's progress over time with our skin diary feature.

K-Beauty Product & Ingredient Tools

Browse curated K-Beauty product recommendations, analyze ingredient lists from text or photo (OCR-powered), and check ingredient compatibility between products. Our database covers over 50 common skincare actives with detailed efficacy and safety information.

Korean Aesthetic Procedure Guide

Detailed information on 15+ popular aesthetic procedures in Korea, including Botox, fillers, laser toning, skin boosters, and more. Each listing includes pricing in KRW and USD, recovery timelines, expected results, and important safety considerations.

Gangnam Clinic Directory

A curated list of reputable dermatology clinics in Gangnam, Sinsa, and Cheongdam — Seoul's top beauty districts. Includes specialties, price ranges, ratings, and direct links to Google Maps and Naver Maps for easy navigation.

Wellness & Daily Tips

Real-time weather-based skincare recommendations tailored to your location, evidence-based nutrient suggestions based on your skin analysis, and the latest K-Beauty trend reports. Plus, curated picks from top Korean beauty YouTubers.
